# Vision

**Tiny Hands** — Extra hands for your Slack workspace.

An open-source, self-hosted AI agent platform powered by Claude. Agents live inside Slack channels, connect to your existing tools and knowledge bases, and execute tasks autonomously through natural conversation.

## Core Value Proposition

- **Your VPS, your keys, your data** — fully self-hosted with no vendor lock-in
- **Agents as team members** — create specialized AI agents that live in Slack and respond contextually
- **Tool & knowledge integration** — connect agents to GitHub, Zendesk, Linear, HubSpot, Google Drive, and more
- **Human-in-the-loop** — approval gates, audit trails, and role-based access for safe autonomous operation
- **Self-improving** — agents propose their own improvements; humans approve before changes take effect

## Target Users

Teams that want AI automation embedded directly in their Slack workspace — customer support, engineering, sales, product, marketing, and operations teams who need intelligent agents that can triage tickets, enrich leads, review PRs, manage knowledge bases, and generate reports.

## Key Differentiators

1. **Slack-native** — agents are first-class Slack citizens, not a separate app
2. **Self-hosted** — deploy on your own infrastructure via Docker Compose or DigitalOcean Marketplace
3. **Multi-agent orchestration** — teams of agents can collaborate on complex tasks
4. **Native documents** — create and edit docs, spreadsheets, and files without leaving the platform
5. **Workflow automation** — define multi-step workflows with waiting states and side effects
